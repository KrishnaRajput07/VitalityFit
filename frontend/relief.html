Push Ups

Type: Strength

Muscle Group: Chest, Triceps

Difficulty: Beginner

Description

Push-ups are a classic exercise to strengthen the chest, shoulders, and triceps. Keep your body straight, core engaged, and lower yourself until your chest nearly touches the floor.

Instructions

  1. Start in a plank position with hands shoulder-width apart.
  2. Lower your body until your chest almost touches the floor.
  3. Push yourself back up to the starting position.
  4. Keep your core tight and body straight throughout.
